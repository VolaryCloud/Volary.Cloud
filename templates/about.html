About VolaryCloud

Learn more about my mission, philosophy, and the developer behind the projects.

The Mission

VolaryCloud was created with a simple but important mission: to develop privacy-respecting, security-focused applications that are accessible to everyone.

In today's digital landscape, privacy is increasingly becoming a luxury rather than a right. Many services collect vast amounts of personal data or hide essential privacy features behind paywalls. I believe this is fundamentally wrong.

That's why every application I develop follows three core principles: it must respect user privacy by design, be security-focused from the ground up, and remain 100% free and open-source.

About the Developer

I'm a 14-year-old developer from Norway who is passionate about programming and digital privacy rights.

My journey in programming began when I was much younger, and I've been developing my skills ever since. What drives me is the belief that technology should empower people, not exploit them.

Being young gives me a unique perspective on building digital tools. I'm growing up in a world where privacy concerns are greater than ever before, which is why I'm committed to creating alternatives that put users first.

Core Values

Privacy by Design

Every application is built with privacy as a fundamental requirement, not as an afterthought. Your data belongs to you alone.

Transparency

Open-source code ensures that all applications are transparent in their operation. You can verify exactly what they do.

Accessibility

All applications are and will always remain free to use. Privacy and security should not be privileges for those who can afford them.
